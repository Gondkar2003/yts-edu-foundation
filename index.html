About YTS EDU Foundation

YTS EDU Foundation is a non-profit organization supported by philanthropists, scientists, and corporates. We identify real talent from schools, coaching 10th-grade students for 2-3 years to help them achieve their dreams.

Our students aim for top institutions like IIT, IIST, IISc, and ISERs. With the support of our advisory board and generous donors, we ensure students receive guidance, resources, and mentorship to reach their full potential.
